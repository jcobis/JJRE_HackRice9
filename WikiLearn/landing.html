WikiLearn

What is WikiLearn?

WikiLearn is a tool that increases the efficiency of your web browsing. How frequently in your browsing to you open a new tab to find something on Wikipedia? With WikiLearn, you can view the Wikipedia entry for anything without even leaving the page you're on.

Usage

To use WikiLearn, simply highlight a word or phrase on a page. If there's a Wikipedia entry for what you highlighted, WikiLearn will show it to you.
