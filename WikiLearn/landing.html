WikiLearn

What is WikiLearn?

WikiLearn is a tool that increases the efficiency of your web browsing by encouraging learning. How frequently do you open new tabs to search for something on Wikipedia? With WikiLearn, you can view the Wikipedia entry for any term without leaving the page you're on. Learon on!

Usage

Simply highlight a word or phrase on a webpage. If there's a Wikipedia entry for what you highlighted, WikiLearn will show it to you in a popup window.
